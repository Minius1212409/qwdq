2. Pet simlator

Move to Pet sim game link

Pet Simulator X, commonly referred to as PSX, is the third iteration of the Pet Simulator series. The game is about
collecting coins & gems to unlock powerful pets. Coins can be used to hatch pets from eggs and purchase new biomes.
New worlds will be unlocked as the player progresses, some of which introduce new game mechanics and features.

Dress To Impress is a Roblox experience where players show off their fashion skills. Created by the Dress To
Impress Group, players compete in themed rounds, customize their avatar, and walk the runway. Players can
rank up from "New Model" to "Top Model".
